# Testimonial Engine

Build Marina's bank of social proof — request, collect, write, and deploy testimonials that show exactly what Ascend Global delivers.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "Do you have existing testimonials to work with, or do we need to request them from past clients?"

## Requesting testimonials

**The right moment:** 24–72 hours after a client's Bali trip concludes — when the impact is fresh and they're back in their normal routine with a new perspective.

**Testimonial request template:**
Short, warm, specific. Ask about the outcome, not the process. "What happened as a result of the introductions Marina made for you?" Not "How did you find working with Marina?"

**What makes a good testimonial for Ascend Global:**
- Names the specific outcome (the introduction, the deal, the investor conversation)
- Captures the before vs. after (what they couldn't access before, what they can now)
- Reflects the emotional quality of the experience — trust, ease, depth
- Is specific enough to be credible, general enough to protect privacy

## Testimonial formats to collect
- Written (for website, email, LinkedIn)
- Video (for Instagram, high-trust situations)
- LinkedIn recommendation (permanent, searchable)

## Output format

**Testimonial Request Email** — ready to send to a specific client.
**Testimonial rewrite** — if Marina has a rough quote, a polished version that retains the client's voice.
**Deployment plan** — where to use each testimonial and in what context.
