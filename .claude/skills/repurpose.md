# Repurpose

Take one anchor piece of Marina's content and extract maximum reach from it — turning it into platform-specific formats without losing voice or diluting message.

## How to run
Ask Marina to share the anchor piece (Substack essay, long caption, voice note, talk excerpt, or key idea). Then build the repurpose package.

## Output — full repurpose package

**1. Instagram caption (short)**
Under 150 words. The one insight that will stop the scroll. Link anchor piece in bio.

**2. Instagram caption (long)**
200–300 words. The full argument, compressed. Earn the read. End with a question.

**3. Instagram carousel concept**
Slide-by-slide structure: Slide 1 = hook, Slides 2–5 = the argument broken into steps or contrasts, Slide 6 = the landing/takeaway. Copy for each slide.

**4. LinkedIn post**
Professional framing of the same core idea. More grounded, slightly less philosophical, still Marina's voice. 150–250 words.

**5. Story sequence (3 frames)**
Frame 1: The hook statement (text on image)
Frame 2: The expansion (one sentence)
Frame 3: The CTA (what to do next)

**6. Email teaser**
Subject line + 3-sentence email that points readers to the anchor piece.

## Rule
Each format must stand alone — not just be a fragment of the original. A person who sees only the carousel should get full value without needing to find the Substack. Repurposing is not copying and pasting.
