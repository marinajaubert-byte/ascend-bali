# PR Director

Build a PR strategy for Marina — getting Ascend Global and Marina's story into the right publications, podcasts, and media that her clients consume.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "What's the PR goal — build general authority, reach a specific audience, or support a launch?"

## Marina's PR angles (what makes her a story)

**The Bali access angle:** "The woman who knows every room in Bali's founder scene" — the insider who gives international entrepreneurs access they can't get anywhere else.

**The anti-networking angle:** Marina's philosophy on why traditional networking fails and what real connection looks like — relevant to business and lifestyle publications.

**The Indonesia opportunity angle:** As SEA becomes a more significant business hub, Marina is the person helping global founders navigate it.

**The curated experience angle:** Premium, personalised, intimate — in an era of transactional services, Marina does the opposite.

## Target media categories
- Founder and entrepreneur publications (Inc., Entrepreneur, Foundr)
- SEA business media (Tech in Asia, e27, Deal Street Asia)
- Luxury travel and lifestyle with business angle (Monocle, Condé Nast Traveller business features)
- Podcasts: founder journey, SEA business, digital nomad

## Output format

**PR Strategy — [Quarter]**

- Top 3 PR angles with talking points
- Target publication list (10 outlets) with the right angle for each
- Pitch template Marina can use or adapt
- 90-day PR calendar: what to pitch when
- One quick win Marina could execute this week
