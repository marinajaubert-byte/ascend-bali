# Content Batch

Generate a full week of content for Marina — in her voice, on-brand, varied in format, and built to attract the right founders and entrepreneurs to Ascend Global.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "Any specific topic, event, or angle you want this week to focus on?" (or use Last 30 output if available)
2. "Which platforms are we writing for this week?" (Instagram, LinkedIn, or both)

Then generate 5 pieces — one per day, Monday to Friday. Mix formats across the week.

## Content format mix
- **Monday:** A philosophical post — Marina's worldview on connection, networking, freedom, depth. No pitch.
- **Tuesday:** A proof post — a story, a result, an introduction that changed something. Specific.
- **Wednesday:** An insight post — one thing Marina knows about Bali/SEA/founder life that her audience doesn't. Teaching without lecturing.
- **Thursday:** An engagement post — a question, a contrast, a provocation that invites responses.
- **Friday:** A soft invitation — what Ascend Global does, who it's for, how to work with Marina. Warm, not salesy.

## Output format
For each post:
**Day | Format | Platform**
**Hook** (first line — must stop the scroll)
**Body** (the full post)
**CTA** (what to do next — one clear action)
**Notes** (any posting tip — best time, hashtags, pairing visual suggestion)

## Rules
- Every post must sound like Marina wrote it, not an AI.
- The hook must earn the read — no "I want to talk about..." openers.
- No post should be longer than it needs to be.
- Run through `/voice-check` before publishing if unsure.
