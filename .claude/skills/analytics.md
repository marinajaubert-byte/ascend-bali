# Analytics

Review Marina's performance data — content, leads, revenue, and engagement — and surface the insights that inform what to do next.

## How to run
Ask Marina to share the numbers she has available — Instagram insights, LinkedIn data, website traffic, lead volume, revenue figures, whatever she tracks.

## What to review

**Content performance:**
- Which posts got the most reach, engagement, saves, and DMs?
- What topic/format is consistently outperforming?
- What is being consistently ignored?

**Lead metrics:**
- How many new inquiries this period?
- Where did they come from?
- What percentage converted to calls? To clients?

**Revenue:**
- Actual vs. target
- Which offer is generating most revenue?
- Average client value this period vs. last

**Audience growth:**
- Follower growth rate — is it accelerating or plateauing?
- Engagement rate trend — are existing followers more or less engaged?

**The signal vs. the noise:**
Most metrics are noise. The signal is: Are the right people finding Marina, and are they converting to clients at a rate that's growing?

## Output format

**Analytics Review — [Period]**

Section-by-section summary with data Marina provided.
**The one number that matters most right now:** The metric that is either the biggest opportunity or the biggest risk.
**Three actions informed by the data:** Specific, not generic.
