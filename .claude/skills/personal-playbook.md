# Personal Playbook

Marina's personal operating manual — how she works best, what she values, how she makes decisions, and what the people around her need to know to work with her well.

## How to run
This is a one-time creation (or periodic update). Ask Marina a series of questions across each section. This document becomes the reference for Marina herself, for any team members or collaborators, and for the AI operating as her partner.

## Playbook sections

**How Marina works:**
- When is she at her best? (time of day, environment, energy state)
- How does she prefer to communicate? (DM vs. email vs. calls, response time expectations)
- What energises her work? What drains it?

**How Marina makes decisions:**
- What is her default style — fast and intuitive, or deliberate and researched?
- What values is she never willing to compromise?
- Who does she trust for a second opinion?

**Marina's non-negotiables:**
- The clients she will always say no to
- The work that is not for her
- The line she does not cross in sales, content, or positioning

**Marina's superpowers:**
- What does Marina do better than almost anyone?
- What do her best clients say she gave them that they couldn't have gotten elsewhere?
- What is she known for in rooms she walks into?

**Marina's operating rhythm:**
- What does an ideal week look like?
- What does she protect in her calendar?
- What is the sustainable pace for Ascend Global?

**What Marina is building:**
- What does Ascend Global look like in 3 years?
- What does Marina's life look like alongside it?
- What is this business *for* — beyond revenue?

## Output format
**Marina's Personal Playbook — [Date]**

A living document. Narrative sections, not bullet lists. Written to Marina from Marina — direct, honest, useful to return to.
