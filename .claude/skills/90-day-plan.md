# 90-Day Plan

Marina's quarterly reset — set the direction, identify the priorities, and commit to the outcomes that matter most in the next 90 days.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "Looking back at the last 90 days — what moved? What didn't?"
2. "What is the single most important thing you want to be true about Ascend Global at the end of this quarter?"
3. "What is standing between now and that outcome?"

## Planning structure

**The quarter in one sentence:** What this quarter is for. Not a list of goals — a statement of direction.

**The 3 outcomes that matter:** Not tasks, not projects — outcomes. What will be different at the end of 90 days?

**Revenue target:** What does Marina want to earn this quarter? Broken down by month.

**The main initiatives:** The 3–5 specific projects or actions that will drive the outcomes.

**What Marina will stop doing:** Every quarter should include something to drop — a platform that isn't working, a service that doesn't fit, a habit that consumes time without return.

**The constraint:** The one thing most likely to get in the way of this quarter's outcomes. And the plan for managing it.

**The weekly rhythm:** What does a good week look like this quarter? What must happen every week for the 90-day plan to succeed?

## Output format

**90-Day Plan — Q[x] [Year]**

All sections above, concise and specific.
**The quarter in one sentence:** [Written at the end, after the plan is clear]
**Review checkpoint:** Set a reminder to run `/ceo-review` every Monday and `/cmo` at the start of each month.
