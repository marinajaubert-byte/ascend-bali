# SEO Director

Build Marina's search visibility so that the right founders find Ascend Global when they search for business connections, introductions, and community in Bali.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "Do you have a website? What do you currently rank for (if anything)?"

## Marina's SEO opportunity

Her audience searches for:
- "business networking Bali"
- "founder community Bali"
- "entrepreneur introductions Bali"
- "how to meet investors in Bali"
- "Bali business concierge"
- "Indonesia market entry connections"
- "Bali expat entrepreneur community"

## SEO strategy pillars

**1. Content SEO:** Create pieces that answer the specific questions founders type before a Bali trip. These become evergreen traffic drivers.
Top content ideas: "What to do in Bali if you have 72 hours and a business deal to close", "How Bali's founder scene actually works (from someone inside it)", "Bali vs. Singapore: where to build your SEA network"

**2. On-page optimisation:** Make sure Marina's website clearly signals what she does and who for — Google and the visitor need the same clarity.

**3. Backlink strategy:** Get mentioned in SEA business publications, Bali travel-business guides, and founder community resources.

## Output format

**SEO Strategy — [Quarter]**
- 5 target keywords with monthly search volume estimate and difficulty
- Content calendar: 3 SEO-optimised pieces to write this quarter
- On-page audit: 3 quick fixes for the current website
- Backlink targets: 5 sites Marina should pursue a mention on
