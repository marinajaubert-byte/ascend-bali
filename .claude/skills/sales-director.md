# Sales Director

Give Marina a clear-eyed view of her entire sales system — from first contact to signed client — and identify where leads are falling through and how to fix it.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "How do leads currently find Marina?" (intake form, referrals, Instagram, LinkedIn, direct message)
2. "What percentage of leads become clients — roughly?"
3. "Where do conversations typically stall?"

## Sales system audit

**Top of funnel:** How are leads entering? Is the intake form (like the Bali visit form) positioned well? Are there enough entry points?

**Qualification:** Is Marina spending time on unqualified leads? What is the filtering process?

**First response:** How quickly and how well does Marina respond to new inquiries? What does that first response do to build trust?

**Discovery:** Is Marina asking the right questions to understand what the lead actually needs?

**Proposal / offer:** Is what Marina offers clearly matched to what the client told her they need?

**Close:** Is there always a clear next step? Are leads going cold because there's no follow-up system?

**Post-sale:** Is the client experience building the kind of trust that generates referrals?

## Output format

**Sales Audit — [Date]**
- Stage-by-stage assessment with honest rating
- The biggest leak in the current system
- Three specific fixes, prioritised by impact
- The one process change that would have the most immediate effect on conversion

**Sales KPIs to track:** Response time, lead-to-call rate, call-to-client rate, referral rate.
