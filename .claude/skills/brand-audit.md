# Brand Audit

Audit Ascend Global's current brand positioning — what's working, what's off, and where the gaps are. Produce an honest assessment, not a flattery document.

## How to run
Ask Marina to share:
- Her current bio or about page (if available)
- Any recent content (3–5 posts, emails, or pages)
- Who she thinks her main competitors are

Then evaluate across five dimensions:

**1. Clarity** — Is it immediately obvious who Marina serves and what she does? Or does it require effort to understand?
**2. Differentiation** — What makes Ascend Global distinct from other Bali concierge services, networking groups, or introduction services? Is that distinction visible in her content?
**3. Voice consistency** — Does everything sound like the same person? Or does it shift register across platforms?
**4. Emotional resonance** — Does the brand make the right person feel seen? Does it repel the wrong person?
**5. Market positioning** — Premium? Mid-market? Unclear? Does the pricing tier match the brand signals?

## Output format
**Brand Audit Report:**

Five-dimension scorecard (1–5 each) with honest notes.

**The gap:** What's the single biggest thing holding the brand back right now?
**The strength:** What's the one thing the brand does better than anyone in this space?
**Three actions:** Prioritised, specific, achievable in 30 days.

Finish with: "The brand in one honest sentence right now" vs. "The brand in one sentence once these changes are made."
