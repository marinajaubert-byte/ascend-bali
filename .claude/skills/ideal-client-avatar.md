# Ideal Client Avatar

Build a detailed, human portrait of Marina's ideal client — specific enough to write for one person, representative enough to reach hundreds.

## How to run
Pull from the CLAUDE.md context and ask Marina to confirm or update:
- What markets are her clients coming from?
- What stage of business are they at?
- What have they tried before finding Marina?

Then build the full profile.

## Output format

**ICA PROFILE — Ascend Global**

**Name & archetype:** [Give them a real name and a one-line archetype, e.g. "David — the Singapore founder opening Asia"]

**Demographics:** Age range, location, business stage, industry

**The trip:** Why are they coming to Bali? What do they hope to achieve in their window of time?

**What they've already tried:** Cold LinkedIn messages, attending public events, Googling "founder community Bali" — what hasn't worked and why?

**What they actually want:** Not just "introductions" — the deeper desire. The market they want to crack. The investor who finally gets what they're building. The partner who makes the deal happen.

**What they fear:** Wasting the trip. Taking another surface-level meeting. Paying for something that doesn't deliver.

**Why they choose Marina:** Trust signals that matter to them. What makes them pick up the phone.

**The words they use:** Quotes in their own voice — how they describe their problem before they've found the solution.

**What success looks like:** The moment they know the trip was worth it.

Keep it human. This is a person, not a demographic spreadsheet.
