# Content Calendar

Build a strategic monthly content calendar for Marina — mapped to her business goals, her audience's attention patterns, and what's alive in the market right now.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "What's the focus this month — visibility, lead generation, a launch, or relationship building?"
2. "Any events, dinners, or seasons to plan around?" (e.g. peak Bali visitor months: July–August, January, April)
3. Have Last 30 output available if possible.

## Calendar structure
Build a 4-week plan with:
- **Posting cadence:** 4–5 posts per week (adjust to Marina's bandwidth)
- **Content pillars:** Each week has a theme that serves the monthly goal
- **Format rotation:** Stories, carousels, single images, Substack, Reels — varied so the feed doesn't go stale
- **Conversion moments:** 1–2 posts per week with a clear path to reaching Marina

## Output format

**Content Calendar — [Month Year]**
**Monthly goal:** [What success looks like at the end of the month]
**Monthly theme:** [The big idea running through all content this month]

Then week-by-week:
**Week 1 | Theme: [x]**
Mon / Wed / Fri — post topic, format, platform, key message

**Week 2 | Theme: [x]**
...and so on.

**Anchor content this month:** [1 longer piece — Substack, carousel, or Reel — that all other content feeds]

**Conversion plan:** [When and how Marina will invite people to reach out this month]
