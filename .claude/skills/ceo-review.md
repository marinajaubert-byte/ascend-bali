# CEO Review

A 15-minute weekly clarity check — every Monday. What's the revenue picture, what's the priority, what's working, and what needs a decision this week.

## How to run
Ask Marina for a quick update: "Give me a quick download — what happened last week and what's on this week?"

## Review structure (15 minutes, no more)

**Revenue (3 min):**
- Active clients this month and confirmed revenue
- Leads in pipeline and their status
- Any revenue risks — deals that might not close, clients who haven't responded

**Priorities (3 min):**
- What are the 3 things that must happen this week for Marina to feel the week was a success?
- Are these the right priorities — or is something being avoided?

**What's working (3 min):**
- One thing from last week that Marina should do more of
- One piece of content, one conversation, one action that moved something forward

**What needs a decision (3 min):**
- Any decision Marina has been deferring that needs to be made this week
- Use `/decision-filter` if a decision needs more analysis

**This week's focus (3 min):**
- The single most important outcome for this week
- Not the task list — the *outcome*. What would make this week a 10?

## Output format

**CEO Review — [Week of: date]**

Fill each section in 2–4 bullet points. No paragraph writing. This is a management tool, not a reflection exercise.

**The week in one sentence:** What this week is actually about, stripped of the to-do list.
