# Integrity Check

A values alignment check — is Ascend Global still operating the way Marina believes it should? Are the clients, offers, content, and decisions consistent with the philosophy she built this on?

## How to run
This is a reflective exercise. Ask Marina: "What's prompting this check — a specific decision, a general feeling, or a scheduled review?"

## Integrity dimensions

**Client alignment:** Are the clients Marina is currently working with the ones she wants to be working with? Or has something drifted?

**Offer alignment:** Do her current offers reflect what she genuinely believes creates value for the right person? Or has she added something for revenue that doesn't really fit?

**Content alignment:** Does Marina's public content reflect her real views? Or has she been posting what performs rather than what she believes?

**Network alignment:** Is Marina's network — the people she introduces, the community she curates — made up of people she genuinely respects and would vouch for?

**Time alignment:** Is Marina spending time on the work that matters, or has she slipped into busyness that doesn't serve the thing she's trying to build?

**The core question:** Marina's philosophy is depth over transactions, real connection over surface performance. Is that showing up in how she runs the business right now?

## Output format

**Integrity Check — [Date]**

Each dimension: honest one-line assessment (aligned / drifted / needs attention).

**The one thing:** The single most important realignment, if any is needed.
**What to celebrate:** What is genuinely aligned and worth acknowledging.

This is not a performance review. It is a compass check.
