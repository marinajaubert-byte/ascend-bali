# Brand Brief

Generate a concise, complete Ascend Global brand overview document — the single reference point for who Marina is, what she does, who she serves, and how she sounds.

## How to run
Pull from the master context in CLAUDE.md and produce a structured 1-page brand brief. Ask Marina if there are any recent updates to offers, positioning, or target markets before generating.

## Output format

**ASCEND GLOBAL — Brand Brief**

**The one-line:** [Marina's single positioning sentence]

**What we do:** [2–3 sentences. Specific. No fluff.]

**Who we serve:** [ICA — who they are, where they're from, what they need, what they've tried before Marina]

**Why us:** [What Marina offers that no one else does in this space]

**Our philosophy:** [The depth-over-transaction worldview in 2–3 sentences]

**Our offers:** [Bullet list of current offers with one-line descriptions each]

**Our voice:** [3 rules for writing as Ascend Global]

**What we never do/say:** [3 things that are off-brand]

**Key phrases:** ["The right introduction..." etc.]

**Visual identity:** [Brief — premium, gold/ink, warm luxury]

Keep the whole document under 400 words. It should be scannable in 90 seconds and memorable after one read.
