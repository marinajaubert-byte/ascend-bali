# Podcast Pitch

Write a compelling podcast guest pitch for Marina — specific to the show, clear on the value for their audience, and written in Marina's voice.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "Which podcast are we pitching — do you have a specific show in mind?"
2. "Have you listened to the show? What episodes do you know?"
3. "What's the angle — Marina's story, the Bali ecosystem, the philosophy of real connection, or something else?"

## What makes a great guest pitch

**For the host, not for Marina:** The pitch must answer "why does my audience need to hear this?" before it answers "who is Marina?"

**Specific episode angle:** Not "I'd love to come on and talk about networking" — a specific episode title, a specific argument, a specific story that will work for that audience.

**Proof:** One line of credibility — not a full bio, the one thing that makes Marina undeniably qualified to speak on this topic.

**Easy yes:** A 2-3 sentence pitch is better than a page. Make it easy to forward to a producer.

## Podcast angles Marina owns
- "Why real connection is the most underrated business strategy"
- "What Bali's founder scene taught me about how relationships actually work"
- "The right introduction vs. the right event — why most entrepreneurs waste their networking budget"
- "Building a business on trust in one of the world's most connected cities"

## Output format
**Subject line** + **Full pitch email** (under 200 words) + **One-sentence bio** for the host to use when introducing Marina.
