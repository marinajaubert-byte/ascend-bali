# Funnel Architect

Design the client journey for Ascend Global — from first discovery to confirmed booking. A funnel for Marina is not a marketing automation sequence; it is a curated path of trust.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "Where are most clients currently coming from — Instagram, LinkedIn, referral, or direct search?"

## Marina's client journey (current and optimised)

**Stage 1 — Discovery:** Founder sees Marina's content, gets referred by a mutual contact, or finds Ascend Global via search. 
What needs to happen: They immediately understand who Marina is and whether they're the right person.

**Stage 2 — Interest:** They explore further — read the website, look at the intake form, see testimonials.
What needs to happen: They build enough trust to reach out.

**Stage 3 — Inquiry:** They fill in the intake form or DM Marina directly.
What needs to happen: Marina responds quickly and personally, showing she read what they shared.

**Stage 4 — Discovery call:** Marina and the lead speak. Fit is assessed on both sides.
What needs to happen: The call ends with a clear next step — a proposal, a confirmed visit plan, or a polite no.

**Stage 5 — Commitment:** The client confirms, pays, and prepares for their Bali visit.
What needs to happen: They feel taken care of from this moment. The experience has already started.

**Stage 6 — Delivery and delight:** The visit happens. Marina delivers.
What needs to happen: Results that exceed what was promised. Referral conversation is natural.

## Output format

**Funnel Map — Ascend Global:**
Each stage: what currently happens vs. what should happen.
Friction points: where leads drop off and why.
Three upgrades: the changes that would have the biggest impact on conversion.
