# Visibility Sprint

A focused 2-week push to get Marina's name and work in front of more of the right people — fast. Not a growth hack. A deliberate effort with real levers.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "What's the trigger for this sprint — upcoming event, slow lead period, new offer launch, or just a general push?"

## Sprint structure (14 days)

**Week 1 — Outbound visibility:**
- 3 pieces of high-quality content on primary platform
- 5 genuine comments on posts from founders, investors, and community builders in SEA (not "great post!" — real engagement)
- 2 personal DMs to warm contacts Marina hasn't spoken to in 90+ days
- 1 collaboration piece (guest post, interview, joint content with someone in Marina's ecosystem)

**Week 2 — Inbound pull:**
- The anchor piece: one definitive piece of content on Marina's most-owned topic (published and shared)
- Repurpose package from anchor piece (use `/repurpose`)
- Follow up any leads generated in Week 1
- Document results for next sprint

## Output format

**Visibility Sprint Plan — [Start date]**

Day-by-day action list for 14 days.
Clear ownership: What Marina does vs. what can be delegated.
The one metric to watch: not vanity metrics — DMs received, profile visits, or warm leads generated.

**Sprint debrief template** (fill at day 14): What moved? What didn't? What to repeat?
