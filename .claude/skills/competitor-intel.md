# Competitor Intel

Map the competitive landscape Marina operates in — who else is connecting founders in Bali, running curated events, offering concierge introductions, or positioning in the SEA entrepreneur space.

## How to run
Search across these competitor categories:

**Direct:** Other Bali-based connectors and concierge services offering introductions for business visitors.
**Adjacent:** Founder communities in Bali (co-working spaces with events, mastermind groups, digital nomad networks).
**Indirect:** Remote networking platforms, LinkedIn premium, paid introduction services (globally).
**Aspirational:** High-end networks Marina's clients might compare her to (YPO, Soho House, Tiger 21).

For each competitor found, document:
- What they offer
- Who they serve
- Their positioning language (the words they use)
- Price signals (if visible)
- What they do well
- Their gap — what they can't offer that Marina can

## Output format

**Competitive Intelligence Report**

| Competitor | Category | Their promise | Their gap | Marina's edge |
|---|---|---|---|---|

**The white space:** What is no one doing that Marina's clients clearly want?

**The positioning move:** One sentence that captures how Marina is different from the entire landscape — not better in a general sense, different in a specific way.

Update this report once per quarter or when a new competitor enters the space.
