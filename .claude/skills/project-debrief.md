# Project Debrief

Capture what happened, what worked, and what to carry forward — after a client visit, event, or major engagement has concluded.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "Which visit or project are we debriefing?"
2. "What went well?"
3. "What could have been better?"
4. "Did the client get what they came for?"

## Debrief structure

**The outcomes:** What actually happened? Which introductions were made? Did the client get what they needed?

**Client experience:** How did the client feel about the experience? Did they express satisfaction, surprise, disappointment? What did they say?

**Network health:** Were the introductions Marina made ones she's proud of? Did both sides of each introduction benefit? Are those relationships something she'd cultivate further?

**Marina's experience:** Was this the right kind of client to work with? Did the engagement feel good to deliver? Would Marina take another client like this?

**What to document:** Any testimonial opportunity? Any case study worth capturing (with permission)? Any lesson that should inform how Marina works in future?

**What to send:** Based on the debrief, what is the most important follow-up action — a thank you, a check-in, a referral request, or simply maintaining the relationship?

## Output format

**Debrief — [Client Name] — [Visit Date]**
Section-by-section summary.
**The one lesson:** The single most important thing this visit taught Marina about her work.
**Next action:** The one thing to do in the next 48 hours.
