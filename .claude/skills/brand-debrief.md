# Brand Debrief

Review a completed campaign or content push — what landed, what didn't, and what the brand learned.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "What campaign or push are we debriefing? What was the goal, and what happened?"

## Debrief structure

**Goal vs. result:** What was the campaign supposed to achieve? What actually happened?

**Brand consistency:** Did the campaign look and sound like Ascend Global throughout? Or did anything feel off-brand under execution pressure?

**Audience response:** How did the right audience respond? More DMs, more profile visits, more lead inquiries? Or engagement from the wrong audience?

**Content performance:** Which pieces of content from the campaign performed best? What did they have in common?

**What surprised Marina:** One thing she didn't expect — positive or negative.

**The lesson for next time:** Not a list of improvements — the single most important thing that will change how Marina runs the next campaign.

**Equity built:** Did this campaign leave the brand stronger or weaker? Did it build trust or spend it?

## Output format

**Brand Debrief — [Campaign Name] — [Date]**

All sections above.
**Campaign verdict:** Did this deserve the Ascend Global name? Yes / With reservations / No.
**The keep:** What to repeat next time.
**The drop:** What to leave behind.
