# Brand Guardian

A fast, strict check before anything goes out under the Ascend Global name. Not a full voice check — a brand alignment check.

## How to run
Take whatever Marina shares and run it through three questions:

**1. Does this represent Ascend Global correctly?**
- Does it communicate premium, intimate, curated?
- Does it feel like it belongs in the same room as Marina's best work?
- Would the right client read this and feel understood?

**2. Could this attract the wrong client or repel the right one?**
- Is anything unclear about who this is for?
- Is there any language that signals mass-market instead of curated?
- Any desperation, over-selling, or discount energy?

**3. Is this honest?**
- Does it promise only what Marina can deliver?
- Is the framing genuine or performative?

## Output
**APPROVED** — with one line on what makes it work.
**APPROVED WITH EDITS** — with the specific edits made inline.
**DO NOT SEND** — with a clear reason and a rewritten version.

Fast. Direct. No softening. Marina's reputation is the brand.
