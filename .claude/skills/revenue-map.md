# Revenue Map

Map all of Marina's current and potential revenue streams — what exists, what could exist, and where the growth is.

## How to run
Ask Marina to list every way she currently earns revenue from Ascend Global. Then identify what's missing.

## Revenue categories to map

**Active revenue (Marina's time = income):**
- Per-visit introduction packages
- Discovery calls with follow-up engagement
- Bespoke concierge day-rates

**Passive/leveraged revenue:**
- Referral arrangements with hotels, venues, visa services, and relocation providers in Bali
- Commission or partnership fees from businesses Marina connects her clients to
- A premium community membership (if applicable)

**Content/authority revenue:**
- Substack paid tier (if launched)
- Sponsored content with aligned Bali brands
- Speaking fees (founder events, SEA business conferences)

**Product revenue:**
- A guide: "Your Business Visit to Bali — Marina's Insider Brief" (high-value PDF or paid guide)
- An online experience: a pre-Bali strategy session for founders planning a visit

## Output format

**Revenue Map — [Date]**

Current revenue streams with monthly estimate.
Potential revenue streams Marina isn't yet tapping.
**The highest-leverage addition:** The one new revenue stream that requires the least new effort and generates the most additional income.
**The dependency risk:** If one stream disappeared tomorrow, what would happen — and how does Marina reduce that risk?
