# Sales Alchemy

Transform a lead into a client — not through pressure or persuasion, but through clarity, trust, and making the right next step obvious.

## Marina's sales philosophy
She doesn't sell. She qualifies, connects, and lets the right clients find their way to yes. The alchemy is in removing the friction from that journey — making sure the person who should work with Marina knows it clearly and knows what to do next.

## How to run
Ask Marina:
1. "Tell me about this lead — where did they come from, what have they shared, and where are we in the conversation?"
2. "What has been their main hesitation or question so far?"
3. "What do they need to believe to move forward?"

## The conversion framework

**Stage 1 — Clarity:** Does the lead understand exactly what Marina offers and what outcome they'll get? If not, the sale isn't happening. Write the message that makes it crystal clear.

**Stage 2 — Fit:** Does Marina actually want to work with this person? Run through `/lead-qualify` if unsure. If they're not the right fit, the most important sales move is saying so honestly.

**Stage 3 — The belief gap:** What does the lead need to believe that they don't yet believe?
- "This will actually open the doors I need" → provide proof (testimonial, case study, specific example)
- "Marina is the person who can do this" → provide authority (story, connection, specific claim)
- "This is worth the investment" → clarify the ROI: what is one right introduction worth to their business?

**Stage 4 — The move:** One clear, specific next step. A call, a proposal, a confirmed date. Never "let me know."

## Output
A specific message or sequence Marina sends to move this lead to the next stage.
