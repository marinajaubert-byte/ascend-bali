# Audience

Map the full landscape of who Marina is speaking to — not just her ideal client, but the whole audience ecosystem around her work.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "What platform or market are we mapping — Instagram, LinkedIn, Substack, or all?" Then build the full picture.

## Output format

**Audience Map — Ascend Global**

**Layer 1 — Buyers (active leads):** Who is currently looking for what Marina offers? What are they searching for? What language do they use?

**Layer 2 — Warm audience (followers who haven't bought yet):** What are they consuming? What beliefs do they need to shift before they'll buy?

**Layer 3 — Cold audience (potential reach):** Who doesn't know Marina yet but would respond to her message? Where do they spend their time online?

**Pain inventory:** List the top 5 frustrations this audience has about networking, Bali, business connections, and finding the right rooms.

**Dream inventory:** List the top 5 outcomes this audience is hoping for from a Bali visit.

**Content triggers:** What topics reliably get this audience to engage, share, or reach out?

**Platform behaviour:** Where does this audience live online? When are they most active? What format works best for them?

Use this map to brief every content and campaign skill. Update when Marina notices audience signals shifting.
