# Last 30

A monthly market pulse scan — what content, topics, formats, and conversations are alive in Marina's niche right now. What's gaining attention. What's fading. What to lean into this month.

## How to run
Search across:
- LinkedIn content from founders and entrepreneur community builders in SEA/Bali
- Instagram content from Bali business and expat community accounts
- Substack and newsletter content relevant to founders, networking, SEA expansion
- Twitter/X conversations around Bali, SEA founders, business introductions, nomad entrepreneurship

Identify:
**Rising topics** — What are people talking about more than last month?
**Declining topics** — What is getting less traction than it was?
**Underserved angles** — What questions are being asked that no one is answering well?
**Viral formats** — What content format is getting disproportionate reach right now?
**Emotional themes** — What fear, frustration, or aspiration is dominating the conversation?

## Output format

**Last 30 — [Month Year]**

**3 rising topics with content angles for Marina:**
Each one: Topic + why it's rising + specific angle for Ascend Global

**1 declining topic to avoid:**
What was working but isn't anymore.

**The underserved question:**
The one thing people are asking that Marina could answer better than anyone.

**This month's content bet:**
One specific recommendation — topic, format, platform — for where Marina should put her energy this month.

Run this at the start of every month before planning content.
