# Offer Check

Audit an existing Ascend Global offer — is it positioned correctly, priced right, and structured to convert the right people?

## How to run
Ask Marina to describe or share the current offer in as much detail as she has: what it includes, what it costs, how it's sold, and how it's been received.

## Audit dimensions

**1. Clarity:** Can someone read the offer and immediately understand what they get, who it's for, and what changes for them? Or does it require explanation?

**2. Desire:** Does the offer speak to what the client actually wants (the outcome) or what Marina provides (the process)? Process is internal. Outcomes belong in the offer.

**3. Price-to-value alignment:** Is the price anchored to what one right introduction is worth to a founder, or to Marina's time? One serious business deal easily justifies a multi-thousand dollar investment in the right introductions.

**4. Ease of yes:** Is the path from interest to payment clear and friction-free? Or does it require multiple back-and-forth steps?

**5. Exclusivity signals:** Does the offer communicate that this is curated, premium, and not available to everyone? Or does it read like a service anyone can buy?

## Output format

**Offer Audit — [Offer Name]**
- Score each dimension (1–5) with honest notes
- The single biggest problem with the offer right now
- Three specific improvements with the updated copy written out
- The offer as it should read after improvements
