# Authority Builder

Build a systematic plan for Marina to become the undisputed connector in the Bali entrepreneur space — the person every founder in Singapore, the US, UAE, and Pakistan thinks of when they plan a business trip to Bali.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "What's your authority goal — to be known in a specific geography, by a specific type of founder, or in a specific sector?"

## Authority levers

**1. Consistent insight** — Regularly publishing genuine market knowledge that only Marina could know. Not generic content. Specific intelligence about Bali's founder ecosystem.

**2. Visible proof** — Making introductions and outcomes public (with permission). "I connected X and Y. Here is what happened." Authority is built on receipts.

**3. Association positioning** — Being seen alongside the right people. Who should Marina be photographed with, interviewed by, or introduced to that signals she is at the level she says she is?

**4. The canonical piece** — One definitive piece of content that becomes the reference point for anyone looking into Bali business connections. A Substack essay, a guide, a thread that gets shared.

**5. Platform presence** — Where does Marina need to show up that she currently doesn't? A podcast? A LinkedIn newsletter? A Substack?

## Output format

**Authority Building Plan — 90 days**

- Current authority level (honest assessment)
- Target authority statement ("In 90 days, Marina is known as...")
- Three specific actions per lever
- The single highest-leverage move in the next 30 days
- Success metrics (not follower count — real signals of authority)
