# Lead Qualify

Help Marina assess whether a lead is genuinely the right fit for Ascend Global — protecting her time, her network, and her reputation.

## How to run
Ask Marina to share what she knows about the lead (from their intake form, DMs, or prior conversation).

## Qualification criteria

**The right fit says yes to all of these:**

1. **Clear purpose:** They know why they're coming to Bali and what they want to achieve. Vagueness ("just to explore") is a risk signal for unmet expectations.

2. **Business readiness:** They are at a stage where the right introduction can actually move something forward — not pre-idea, not pre-revenue with no traction.

3. **Time-appropriate:** Their visit timing gives Marina enough time to prepare meaningful introductions (less than 48 hours notice is a challenge).

4. **Respectful of the process:** They understand that Marina's value is in quality curation, not volume. A lead who wants "as many meetings as possible" regardless of quality may not respect the network.

5. **Aligned on investment:** They are not looking for a free service or a dramatically underpriced engagement.

6. **Cultural fit:** Marina works with people she would be comfortable introducing to her best contacts. If a lead feels off — in any direction — that matters.

## Output format

**Lead Qualification Assessment — [Name]**

Score each criterion (green/amber/red) with a one-line note.

**Verdict:** Ideal fit / Work with conditions / Not the right fit

**If amber or red:** One thing Marina could clarify on a call before deciding.
**If not the right fit:** A polite, honest message Marina can send that leaves the door open for the future without wasting either party's time.
