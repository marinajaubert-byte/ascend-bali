# CMO

Marina's monthly strategic marketing question — what to push, what to drop, and the single most important move this month to grow Ascend Global.

## How to run
Use Last 30 output if available. Ask Marina: "What's the business goal this month — revenue, visibility, a specific launch, or building pipeline?"

## Monthly CMO review

**Market pulse:** What is the conversation right now in Marina's space? What are founders talking about? What's happening in Bali's business community? (Pull from `/last30`)

**Content effectiveness:** What has performed best in the last 30 days? What fell flat? Why?

**Pipeline health:** How does the lead pipeline look? Is Marina booked out for upcoming months? Are there gaps to fill?

**The strategic question:** The one question Marina should answer this month to move the business forward. Not operational ("what should I post?") — strategic ("should I launch a community tier?" "Is LinkedIn worth the time I'm giving it?" "Is the current offer mix right?").

**The big move:** One specific, high-leverage action Marina should take this month that she hasn't been taking. This is the CMO's job — not to confirm what Marina's already doing, but to push her to the next level.

## Output format

**CMO Brief — [Month Year]**

Market pulse summary.
Top performer from last month + lesson.
Pipeline assessment.
The strategic question — with Marina's current thinking reflected back, and a push.
The big move — specific, actionable, with a reason why now.
