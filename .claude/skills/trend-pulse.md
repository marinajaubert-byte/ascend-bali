# Trend Pulse

Track the macro trends shaping Marina's world — Bali as a business hub, SEA founder ecosystem growth, the shift in how entrepreneurs network, remote work patterns, Indonesia's regulatory environment, and the global movement of capital and founders.

## How to run
Scan across:
- SEA startup and founder ecosystem news
- Indonesia business and investment news
- Global nomad and remote work trends
- Shifts in how founders use physical spaces for business (Bali specifically)
- Changes in how high-net-worth individuals travel for business purposes
- Platform trends affecting Marina's content and visibility

## Output format

**Trend Pulse — [Date]**

**3 macro trends Marina should be aware of:**
For each: What is the trend, what's the evidence, and what does it mean for Ascend Global?

**1 trend to act on now:**
The single most actionable trend for Marina's business in the next 90 days.

**1 trend to watch:**
Something early-stage that could matter significantly in 12–18 months.

**The opportunity signal:**
What does the trend landscape reveal about a gap Marina could position into?

Run this quarterly or whenever Marina senses the market shifting.
