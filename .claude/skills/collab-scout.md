# Collab Scout

Find and evaluate collaboration opportunities for Marina — people, brands, and platforms whose audience overlaps with hers and who could amplify Ascend Global in the right direction.

## How to run
Ask Marina: "What are we looking for — content collaborations, event partnerships, referral arrangements, or co-created offers?"

## What makes a good collaboration for Marina

**Audience fit:** Their audience includes founders, entrepreneurs, or investors who visit or want to visit Bali/SEA. Marina's audience gets introduced to someone valuable; the partner's audience gets introduced to Marina.

**Brand alignment:** The collaborator is premium, genuine, and respected. No mass-market brands, discount energy, or misaligned values.

**Reciprocal value:** Both sides genuinely benefit — not just Marina getting exposure, or Marina giving away free value.

**Categories to search:**
- High-end travel and relocation services for entrepreneurs
- Singapore/UAE/UK founder communities with SEA expansion interest
- Business podcasts where Marina's audience listens
- Bali-based luxury hospitality brands (hotels, private villas, restaurants)
- Immigration and visa services for international entrepreneurs in Indonesia
- Mastermind groups and founder retreats with SEA components

## Output format

**Collab Scout Report**

5 specific collaboration opportunities with:
- Who / what they are
- Why their audience is Marina's audience
- The collaboration format (podcast guest, joint event, referral arrangement, co-created content)
- How to approach them
- Effort required vs. likely return

**Top pick:** The one to pursue first, with a brief reason why.
