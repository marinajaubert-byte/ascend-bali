# Lead Reply

Write Marina's first response to a new inbound lead — warm, personal, and specific to what they shared.

## How to run
Ask Marina to share what the lead sent or filled in. Then write the response.

## Response principles

**Respond fast:** Leads are warmest within the first hour. Marina's reply should feel like she was already thinking about them.

**Show you read it:** Reference something specific they shared. Not a summary — one specific detail that shows genuine attention.

**Reflect their goal back:** "You mentioned you're looking to meet investors who understand SaaS in Indonesia — that's exactly the kind of introduction that makes a Bali visit worth the flight."

**Establish Marina's credibility without performing it:** One sentence that signals she knows this world and has done this before. Not a list of credentials — a single line of authority.

**Make the next step clear and easy:** Propose a specific call time or ask one specific question to move the conversation forward. Never "let me know if you'd like to chat."

## Output format
**Reply to [Lead Name]:**

Full draft, ready to send.
Subject line if email.
Tone note: how formal/warm to pitch this specific response based on how they wrote.

**Optional P.S.:** A single line that can go at the bottom — a relevant detail, a recent connection Marina made in their sector, something that signals this isn't a template.
